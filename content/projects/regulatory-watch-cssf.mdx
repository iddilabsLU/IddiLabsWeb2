## Problem

- New circulars, FAQs, and regulatory updates appear without warning
- Manual monitoring is time-consuming and easy to miss
- Impact assessment requires reading lengthy technical documents
- Teams waste hours tracking changes across multiple CSSF pages

**Pain points:**
- No centralized alert system for CSSF updates
- Initial impact classification is subjective and inconsistent

- Response time suffers when teams learn about updates late
