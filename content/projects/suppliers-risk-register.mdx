## Problem

Financial institutions in Luxembourg face increasing pressure to maintain comprehensive supplier registers that comply with CSSF Circular 22/806 and EBA Guidelines on ICT and Security Risk Management (EBA/GL/2019/02).

**Key challenges:**
- Manual tracking in spreadsheets is error-prone and hard to audit
- Criticality scoring lacks consistency across teams
- Due diligence documentation is scattered
- Regulatory reporting requires significant manual effort
