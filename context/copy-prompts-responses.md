## 2. About Section

**Prompt:**

> 3–4 sentences describing IddiLabs, 3 principles, and a closing line.

**Response:**
IddiLabs is an independent initiative exploring how AI can simplify compliance, risk, and operational work.
Each project tests a small, real-world idea — built without commercial intent, for learning and transparency.
Every tool emphasizes privacy, clarity, and accessibility.
Based in Luxembourg, IddiLabs experiments with ways to make governance more efficient and human-friendly.

**Principles:**

* Privacy-first, transparent by design.
* Small, testable tools over complex systems.
* Knowledge shared openly whenever possible.

**Closing line:** Ideas become tools, and tools become learning.

## 4. Projects — Intro

**Prompt:**

> Section intro and summaries for each core project.

**Response:**
**Intro:** Practical AI experiments for governance and operations — small tools, simple goals, transparent methods.

**Projects:**

**1. Suppliers Risk Register**
Offline, open-source supplier register aligned with CSSF 22/806 and EBA guidelines. Tracks risk levels, due diligence, and contract data.

**2. Transcripts to AI Minutes**
Local processor converting meeting transcripts into structured minutes with decisions, actions, and responsibilities.

**3. Luxembourg ChatBot**
An AI assistant that answers everyday questions about life and work in Luxembourg, built with multilingual awareness.

**4. Custom AI Assistant (Offline LLM)**
On-premises assistant for regulated entities. Runs offline and trains on internal documents securely.

**5. Regulatory Watch (CSSF Digest)**
Automatic monitoring of new CSSF publications with summaries and impact classifications.
